Mind-Brain 脈德小腦瓜

React + TypeScript 全端思考管理應用 | 2024.11 - 持續開發中 React + TypeScript Full-Stack App | Nov 2024 - Ongoing

這不只是一個專案，而是我對「如何讓科技真正觸動人心」的實踐。從設計到開發，每一個功能都在思考：如何能給忙碌的現代人一個溫暖的數位空間，讓他們慢下來、整理思緒、感受內在的平靜？ This is not just a project, but my practice of "how to make technology truly touch people's hearts." Every feature considers: how can we give busy modern people a warm digital space to slow down and find inner peace?

目標使用者 Target Users
