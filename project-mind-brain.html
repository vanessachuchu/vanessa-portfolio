React + TypeScript 全端思考管理應用 | 2024.11 - 持續開發中 React + TypeScript Full-Stack App | Nov 2024 - Ongoing

這不只是一個專案，而是我對「如何讓科技真正觸動人心」的實踐。從設計到開發，每一個功能都在思考：如何能給忙碌的現代人一個溫暖的數位空間，讓他們慢下來、整理思緒、感受內在的平靜？我用 React + TypeScript 建構了完整的前後端系統，但技術只是手段——真正的目標是創造一個讓人感到被理解的產品。 This is not just a project, but my practice of "how to make technology truly touch people's hearts." From design to development, every feature considers: how can we give busy modern people a warm digital space to slow down, organize thoughts, and feel inner peace? I built a complete front-end and back-end system with React + TypeScript, but technology is just the means—the real goal is to create a product that makes people feel understood.

專案背景 Project Background

在快節奏的現代生活中，人們常常被各種資訊和壓力包圍，很難有機會停下來思考和反省。脈德小腦瓜誕生於對內心平靜的渴望，以及對深度思考價值的重新認識。 In the fast-paced modern life, people are often surrounded by information and pressure, with few chances to stop and reflect. Mind-Brain was born from a desire for inner peace and a renewed appreciation for the value of deep thinking.

這個專案結合了冥想的理念與互動式設計，提供一個安全、溫暖的數位空間，讓使用者能夠慢下來，與自己的內心對話。 This project combines meditation philosophy with interactive design, providing a safe, warm digital space where users can slow down and dialogue with their inner selves.

核心特色 Core Features

  • 互動式思考引導，幫助用戶深入探索內心想法 Interactive thinking guidance to help users explore inner thoughts deeply
  • 溫暖的視覺設計，營造安全舒適的思考環境 Warm visual design creating a safe and comfortable thinking environment
  • 簡潔的操作流程，降低使用門檻 Simple operation process lowering the barrier to use
  • 開源設計，促進社群參與和改進 Open source design promoting community participation and improvement

設計理念 Design Philosophy

「在這個快速變化的世界裡，我們都需要一個安靜的角落，來傾聽自己內心的聲音。」 "In this rapidly changing world, we all need a quiet corner to listen to our inner voice."

慢下來的勇氣 The Courage to Slow Down

在追求效率的時代，我們提倡慢思考的價值。給自己時間去感受、去思考、去理解，這並不是浪費時間，而是對自己最好的投資。 In an era pursuing efficiency, we advocate for the value of slow thinking. Giving yourself time to feel, think, and understand is not a waste of time, but the best investment in yourself.

具體化抽象 Materializing the Abstract

透過互動式的引導，幫助用戶將模糊的感受和想法轉化為清晰的認知，讓內心的聲音變得可見、可感、可理解。 Through interactive guidance, help users transform vague feelings and thoughts into clear cognition, making inner voices visible, tangible, and understandable.
